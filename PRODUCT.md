# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Electricians, contractors, hardware shop owners, and homeowners in and around Sitamarhi, Bihar. They visit the site on mobile phones, often on slower connections, looking for a wholesale electrical supplier they can trust. Their context: comparing prices, finding directions, or calling to inquire about stock. Hindi is their primary language; English is secondary.

## Product Purpose

A single-page landing site for S. K. Electronics, an electrical appliance wholesaler on Ring Bandh Road, Sitamarhi. The site drives two actions: phone calls and in-store visits. Success = a visitor calls +91 90654 73333 or navigates to the shop via Google Maps. The site establishes credibility (4.9 rating, 160+ ratings), communicates the product range (fans, lighting, fittings, power backup), and makes contact effortless. Bilingual English + Hindi throughout.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy, straightforward, established. Three words: reliable, local, professional. The feel of walking into a well-stocked shop where the owner knows your name. Not slick, not corporate. Warm competence.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with purple gradients and glassmorphism
- Over-designed tech startup sites that feel foreign to Sitamarhi
- Sites that are English-only or Hindi-only (must be bilingual)
- Heavy animations or effects that slow down on budget phones

## Design Principles

1. **Trust over flash**. A 4.9 rating and clear phone number beat a fancy animation. Every element should reinforce reliability.
2. **Mobile-first reality**. Most visitors are on Android phones with 3-4G connections. Keep it light, fast, and touch-friendly.
3. **Bilingual by default**. Hindi and English exist side by side, not in separate sections. Both are first-class.
4. **One clear action**. Call or visit. Every section funnels toward one of these two outcomes.
5. **Local pride**. This is a Sitamarhi business. The address, the map, the language — it all says "we're here, we're local, we're open."

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Hindi readers should have equal access. Touch targets minimum 44px. High contrast for outdoor readability on phones. Respects reduced motion.
